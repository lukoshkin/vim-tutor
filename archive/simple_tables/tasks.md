## task-b

Create a table with items that range: from (1, 0) to (100, 0) - 1st column
and (1, 1) to (100, 1) - 2nd column.

---

  | column 0 | column 1 |
  |:--------:|:--------:|
  | (1,   0) | (1,   1) |
  | (100, 0) | (100, 1) |
